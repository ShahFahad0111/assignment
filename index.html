Kumrat Valley

Kumrat Valley is located in the Upper Dir District of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan. Known for its towering Deodar trees, crystal-clear Panjkora River, and untouched wilderness, it's ideal for those seeking peace and natural beauty. The valley remains relatively unexplored, preserving its serenity.

Jahaz Banda

Jahaz Banda is a stunning meadow in the Kumrat region, accessible through a hiking trail from Thal. It sits at an elevation of 3,100 meters and offers panoramic views of surrounding peaks. It's a perfect site for overnight camping under a sky full of stars.

Katora Lake

Located near Jahaz Banda, Katora Lake is a glacial lake shaped like a bowl ("katora" in Urdu). Reaching it involves a moderate hike through pine forests, alpine meadows, and streams. The lake reflects surrounding snow-covered peaks, creating an otherworldly scene.

Styling with Tailwind CSS

Tailwind CSS is a utility-first CSS framework that allows you to build modern designs fast. Instead of writing custom CSS, you apply pre-built classes directly to HTML elements. For example, a button: 

<button class="bg-blue-500 text-white px-4 py-2 rounded">
  Click Me
</button>

Tailwind is excellent for responsive, mobile-first designs and works great with modern front-end tools.

Deploying Websites with GitHub Pages

GitHub Pages lets you host static websites for free directly from a repository. To set it up:

  • Create a public repository
  • Upload your HTML/CSS files
  • Go to Settings > Pages
  • Choose the branch (e.g., main) and folder (usually /root)

Your site will be live at https://yourusername.github.io/reponame.
